Business is about people

All businesses want to turn visitors into recurring accounts, to do this the key is social engagement.Responding to chats, queries, complaints etc. can tremendously help to increase number of repeat customers or better into lifetime accounts.
Socioboard empowers its users to effectively engage their audience across numerous platforms.

Smart inbox of Socioboard is your epicenter of customer interaction.All the public posts by a user and much more would be featured on the message page along with the message picture.Users can view tasks and archived messages easily.When you click Smart Inbox, It displays all messages which are available on Facebook, LinkedIn and Twitter.You can use sort function of Profile and Message type. According to this functionality you can see particular user’s message. There are buttons available, to allot tasks, to comment on a particular post, to archive a message.

Socioboard offers advanced scheduling and publishing.Publishing data is important part of engaging audience through social media platforms, through Socioboard’s publishing tool users can schedule messages, maintain a queue and save drafted messages. You can’t miss any news about what your friends are doing having a dedicated page for notifications. Mass import facility is also available through an excel file.

Feeds empower you to see stuff from pals and partners, all at one place. Users can get wall posts, statuses etc. and respond to them quickly without going through hassle of attending to things separately.In this age of information overload this functionality helps to speed things up and perform quality engagement. Users can get notifications on missed messages and much more.

Collaboration on tasks is an important aspect of social engagement. Collaboration is a very important term in social media marketing, cross-functional departments can collaborate with each other to make the marketing experience more fruitful and synergistic. Socioboard comes up with a marvelous functionality by virtue of which users can make groups & reach out to each other using a highly efficient collaboration mechanism. It can serve as a platform to share information with other team members and increase workability and efficiency of all teams.
